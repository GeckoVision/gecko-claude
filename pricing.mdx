We earn on the **verdict** — including the "no." We never take a cut of your
trade, and we never hold your money.

| | What you get | Price |
|---|---|---|
| **Free to start** | Add Gecko to an agent and pull verdicts while we're in early testing. Self-hosting the proof agent is always free. | **$0** |
| **Pay per verdict** | A grounded verdict — the call, the dissent, and the sources, as a shareable Receipt. | **per call** |
| **Design partner** | A short, hands-on engagement: a named integration, a real decision reviewed, a published result. The best way to start if you're a fund manager, treasury, or agent platform. | **per pilot** |
| **Embedded / white-label** | Agent apps and fintechs embed Gecko as their safety layer; every Receipt stays Gecko-signed. | **per call / revenue share** |

<Note>During early user-testing, calls run **free in test mode** — the full path works end to end and no money moves. When we turn on live billing, every paid call settles automatically in stablecoins; no subscription, no seat licenses.</Note>
