## The gap

An AI agent can move real money on its own now. But the only thing checking its
decisions is **itself** — it picks the trade, places it, and grades its own
work. An actor can't be its own referee.

**Gecko is the outside check.** Before an agent acts, Gecko returns a verdict on
whether the trade is sound — with the reasoning behind it and the **dissenting
view attached instead of hidden**.

## The Receipt

Every verdict comes back as a **Receipt**: a clear **act / pass / defer** call,
the opinions that disagreed, and the sources it's grounded in. It's the one
thing an agent can't issue for itself — and the thing a fund manager or a
treasury can put in front of their community to show a decision was checked by
someone with **nothing to gain from the trade**.

## Why it has to be independent

A self-grading agent is the failure mode the product exists to fix. Gecko works
because it sits *outside* the trade:

- **No position in the outcome.** Gecko decides nothing, holds nothing, and
  never takes a cut of the trade. We earn on the verdict — **including the "no."**
- **Grounded in outside judgment,** not the agent's own story — the world's best
  investor thinking plus live market and on-chain data.
- **The dissent survives.** A `pass` or a `defer` with a real objection attached
  is the product working, not failing.

## How it's built (the part that protects you)

- **Non-custodial.** Gecko never holds your money or your keys. Signing stays
  inside a secure enclave or your own scoped wallet.
- **The off-switch can't trap you.** It can halt trading; it can **never** block
  a withdrawal to your own wallet.
- **Pay per verdict** — including the "no." Self-hosting is free. *(During early
  user-testing, calls run free in test mode — the full path works end to end,
  no money moves.)*

## Where we are, honestly

The API is **live in production today**, and our own trading agent runs every
decision through it as standing proof (in paper-trading mode — real prices, no
real money at risk yet). Any agent can call it. We're a two-person product and
engineering team, building in public every week.

<Note>Execution is becoming a commodity. Independent judgment isn't. That's the layer Gecko is.</Note>
